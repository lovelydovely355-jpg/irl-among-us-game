Role & Modifier
Operator Manual

This is for the hostless, in-person version. It distinguishes what the laptop can enforce from what players must carry out honestly in real life. The app never knows where people are unless someone enters a room while recording an action.

App-run / app-recordedPlayer-run physical ruleAutomatic

Universal no-host flow

1. Private accessPress PLAYER CONSOLE, select your name, and type your private 3-digit PIN.
2. Use an abilityBlue button = role. Tap a player card, choose room/role only when asked, then confirm. Yellow button = modifier.
3. Log real eventsRed Log safe elimination records a role kill. For an offline/real-life action, use public Mark out and enter room + eliminator.
4. Report / meetingREPORT BODY now asks for the reporter’s PIN, then body selection. Emergency Meeting opens a meeting; every living player privately casts a vote.
Guardian Angel / Medic exact process: owner logs in → blue role button → taps target → Apply shield. When target is logged out, someone presses REPORT BODY → enters their own PIN → taps that body. The app publicly announces the save and restores that player.
Physical-only roles are still hostless, but not computer-policed: they have a timer, instruction, public log, or paper card. Everyone must follow the group rule. The app cannot prove where someone walked, what they saw, whether they covered a name tag, or whether a ghost answered honestly.

Important boundaries and answers

  • No human host is required. Each ability is initiated by its owner entering their own PIN. The app handles hidden target selection, timers, effects, bodies, reports, vote math, and role checks wherever possible.
  • Manual Mark out is the backup. Use it when something occurred away from the laptop, such as a Sheriff action performed physically. Put in the correct room and killer when known so Bait, Oracle, Celebrity, Diseased, Risk Taker, and protective effects can resolve correctly.
  • Only logged actions become evidence. Tracker, Spy, Investigator, Lookout, Mystic, Detective, and Sleuth use app records; they do not magically track live rooms.
  • Primary roles and modifiers are separate. Exact-role guess abilities guess a player’s main role, not their modifier.
  • End Game / Reveal Roles awards Survivor, eligible Opportunist, and linked-lover survival results, then shows the complete role list.
